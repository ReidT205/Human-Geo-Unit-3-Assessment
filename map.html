Key Locations

Navajo Nation, USA

The origin of indigenous patterns (woven designs) that are frequently appropriated by fashion brands without permission or credit. These designs have deep cultural and spiritual significance to the Navajo people.

Paris, France

A global fashion capital where designers often showcase appropriated cultural elements on runways, influencing worldwide trends. Paris Fashion Week has featured multiple instances of cultural appropriation that sparked controversy.

H&M Stores Globally

Represents fast-fashion retail where appropriated designs are mass-produced and sold, spreading cultural symbols globally without proper context or acknowledgement.

Instagram

Platform where appropriation is displayed (influencer outfits) and debated (protest hashtags), shaping global cultural discourse about fashion and appropriation.

New York Fashion Week, USA

A significant event where designers present collections, sometimes featuring appropriated cultural symbols detached from their roots. These shows influence global fashion trends.
